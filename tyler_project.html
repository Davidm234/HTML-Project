About Tyler, The Creator

Tyler, The Creator is an American rapper, singer,songwriter, record producer, and music video director. Known for his unique style and artistic vision, Tyler has made a significant impact on the music industry with his groundbreaking albums and innovative projects. Tyler first gained attention as the co-founder of the alternative hip-hop collective Odd Future. His debut album Goblin (2011), established his gritty, controversial style. As his career progressed with albums Wolf (2013) and Cherry Bomb (2014), Tyler began experimenting with more eclectic sounds, by blending jazz, R&B, and rock into his music. Tyler experienced a breakthrough with Flower Boy (2017), showcasing his melodic and introspective style. His album IGOR (2019) won a Grammy for Best Rap Album, displaying his genre-defying creativity. Call Me If You Get Lost (2021) solidified his reputation as a versatile artist, blending hip-hop, soul, and funk with themes of personal growth and self-reflection.
